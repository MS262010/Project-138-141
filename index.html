AI Ping Pong Game





Made by : Mandrita Sinha

Instructions


1. Keep your computer screen straight.
2. Move yourself approximately 3-4 feet away from the screen.
3. Move your wrist in an ascending and descending manner. A red dot should appear on your right wrist.
4. Now press the play button and again, move yourself 3-4 feet away from the computer.
5. The red paddle is yours and will move according to the movement of your right wrist.
